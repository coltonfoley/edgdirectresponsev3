## Image Optimization Tips

1. **Format:** Use `.jpg` for photos, `.png` for graphics with transparency
2. **Size:** Compress images before uploading (use [Squoosh](https://squoosh.app/) or similar)
3. **Naming:** Use lowercase with hyphens (e.g., `pergola-hero.jpg`)
4. **Quality:** Aim for 80-85% quality JPEG compression for best size/quality balance
