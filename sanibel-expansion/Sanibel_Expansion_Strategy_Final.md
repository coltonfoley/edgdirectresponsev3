# Sanibel Island Market Expansion Strategy

## Executive Summary
This document outlines the strategy for entering the Sanibel Island, FL market with a focus on high-end outdoor living structures (pergolas, shading, enclosures). The approach is **digital-first**, leveraging local builder partnerships for execution, avoiding overhead "boots on the ground" while maintaining a local digital presence.

**Core Positioning:** "Luxury systems built to code."
**Differentiation:** Engineering-led transparency, bespoke design capabilities, and brand-agnostic product curation.

---

## 1. Regulatory Landscape
Sanibel Island has one of the strictest building codes in Florida due to its coastal location and conservation ethos.

### Key Regulations
*   **Florida Building Code (FBC)**: Adopts the 2023 (8th Edition).
*   **Permitting**: Required for any work >$750.
*   **Pre-Requisite**: Planning Division review is mandatory *before* Building Division review.
*   **Coverage Limits**: Strict "impermeable coverage" limits. Pergolas/lanais count towards this. 
*   **Flood Zones**: Compliance with FBC Residential Section R322 is mandatory for accessory structures in flood hazard areas.

### The "Impervious Coverage" Strategy
*   **The Problem**: Sanibel has strict limits on how much of a lot can be covered by water-blocking surfaces (houses, driveways, pergolas). Many luxury homes are already "maxed out," meaning they legally cannot add a shade structure.
*   **The Solution (The "Trade-Off")**: By replacing an existing concrete patio (impervious) with specific **pervious pavers** (which Sanibel counts as 100% permeable), we "free up" square footage in the eyes of the city.
*   **The Unlock**: This "found" coverage allowance can then be applied to the new pergola. We don't just sell the roof; we provide the *regulatory path* to build it.

## 4. Digital Market Entry

### Google My Business (GMB) Strategy
*   **Status**: Google prohibits P.O. Boxes and Virtual Offices in 2025.
*   **Primary Approach (Service Area Business)**: Register as a "Service Area Business" (SAB). Verify at a real address (e.g., partner base) but **hide the address** publicly. Set "Sanibel" as the service area.
*   **Reach Goal (Partner Co-Location)**: Negotiate a permanent signage presence at a top builder's location to pass video verification for a pinned map location.

### SEO & Content Strategy
*   **Keywords**: "Miami-Dade Rated Pergolas Sanibel", "Sanibel Permittable Lanais", "Pervious Paver Patio Sanibel".
*   **Lead Magnet**: *"The Sanibel Homeowner's Guide to Outdoor Coverage Limits"* — explaining how to use the paver workaround to legally add luxury structures.
